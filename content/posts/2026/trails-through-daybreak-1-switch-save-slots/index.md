If you were ever wondering, how many saves can I have in total on the Nintendo Switch version of "The Legend Of Heroes: Trails Through Daybreak" (first game): **it's 29**.

It shows 29 on the save screen, and if you exceed that, you can no longer create more saves and have to override your old ones.

No, it's not a bug when you hit 29 and you find you can't create any more saves. The limit is 29.

Hope this helps somebody out there.
